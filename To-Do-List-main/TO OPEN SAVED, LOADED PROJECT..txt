TO OPEN SAVED / LOADED PROJECT.

1 OPEN INTELLIJ.
2 PRESS THE THREE LINES ON TOP(more).
3 PRESS FILE->NEW->OPEN FROM EXISTING SOURCES.
4 WHILE SELECTING PROJECT, SELECT IMPORT AND SELECT SBT OPTION.
5 WAIT FOR THE FILES TO LOAD.
6 LOOK FOR SBT IN THE RIGHT BAR UNDER DATABSE.
7 SELECT A CONFIGURATION.
8 SELECT PLAY AND DO THE REGULAR EDITS.
9 RUN.
10 THEPROJECT SHOULD RUN WITHOUT ANY ERRORS.
